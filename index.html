Cooperative Climbing Adventure

Peak Game is a cooperative third-person adventure game where players scale dangerous mountains, solve environmental puzzles, and forge deep connections through the power of teamwork.

Set in a mysterious, post-cataclysmic world where ancient mountains hide the remnants of forgotten civilizations, players take on the roles of two climbers seeking truth—and each other—amid the silence of the peaks.

Genre
Adventure / Puzzle
Players
1-2 Cooperative
Platform
PC, Mac, Linux
Age Rating
16+

Realistic Climbing Mechanics

Experience authentic mountaineering with realistic rope physics, momentum-based traversal, and dynamic weather systems that affect your climbing routes and tactics.

Master synchronous climbing mechanics where every move requires coordination with your partner. Balance, timing, and trust are essential for survival.
